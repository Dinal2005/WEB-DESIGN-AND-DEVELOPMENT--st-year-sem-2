Importance Of Chemistry


Chemistry is a very important area of study. The study of matter's building blocks, such as atoms, molecules, and ions, as well as its properties, structures, behaviours, and interactions, is known as chemistry. Due to the fact that everything is made up of atoms and molecules, we can witness chemistry everywhere. It also discusses the characteristics of these chemicals as well as the reactions that take place when they mix to form new substances.
